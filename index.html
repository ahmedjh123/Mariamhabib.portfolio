About Me

I am an Urban Planning student at Malmö University, specializing in accessibility and social integration. I am passionate about creating inclusive urban environments, particularly for people with disabilities.

Through my work, including GIS analysis and urban design, I strive to make cities more accessible and equitable for everyone. My vision is to create urban environments where all people, regardless of physical conditions, can move freely and participate in city life.
